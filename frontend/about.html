Plant Disease Analyzer

The Plant Disease Analyzer is an AI-based web application designed to detect plant leaf diseases using image processing and machine learning techniques.

Users can upload a plant leaf image, and the system automatically identifies the disease along with recommended treatment methods.

Project Objectives

  • Early detection of plant diseases
  • Reduce crop damage
  • Support farmers with AI technology
  • Improve agricultural productivity
